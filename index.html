Jhumpa Simulator

Interpreter of Maladies by Jhumpa Lahiri is a combination of nine different short stories.

Throughout the different short stories, many marital problems in characters arise, though Jhumpa Lahiri was in fact never married when the novel was written.

These marital problems are in fact results of Lahiri's mind attempting to repress her fears of marriage as she is writing the short stories.

Take control of Jhumpa's mind and repress feelings in Jhumpa Simulator. Use any key to type, and when a core issue arises, use the buttons to repress them at just the right time.

Win a pulitzer with four or more stars. Lose by taking over 10 years to write nine short stories.

For the best experience, please use Google Chrome on a standard screen size. This game does not work on mobile.
